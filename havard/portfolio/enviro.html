ENVIRO

UI/UX Case Study

Overview

This a platform that I created as a case study when interviewing for a job. The application promotes the existing environmental club at Open Institute University to students and sponsors. The students can view events and register to attend. The administration on the other end will regulate and monitor the users.


Challenge

Designing a platform that will promote the existing environmental club at Open Institute University to students and sponsors


My Role

I am the UI/UX designer of this project


Responsibilities

Conducted interviews, wireframing, prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.

Usability study

Study type: Moderated usability study.

Location: Remote.

Lenght: 50 minutes.

Participants: 5 parcipants (Two admins and three students).


Study findings




Forum

Users need a forum to engage with like minded people within the app



Events

Users need an events reminder feature.



Users

Ease to activate and deactivate users from the admin side.

Improving designs

Based on the insights from the usability studies, I applied design changes and came up with the mockups and high fidelity prototype. I ensured accessibility throughout the design.


Accessibility considerations

  • Used constrasting colors to ensure the website is visually usable to individuals with low vision or varying levels of color blindness.
  • Proper use of hierachy and layout
  • Use of proper typography
